COOKIE POLICY






1 Cookie Policy

  • Purpose:

    To explain how we use cookies and similar technologies, ensuring compliance with UAE and international privacy laws, including the PDPL and GDPR.

  • Policy:

    1. Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for core website functionality, such as login and transaction processing.

    2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymized data for performance insights and traffic analysis.

    1. Cookie Management:
      Users can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the cookie consent banner or browser settings.

    2. Opt-Out Mechanisms:
      Users may opt-out of advertising cookies using third-party tools like Google Ads Preferences Manager.

  2. Retention of Data Collected via Cookies:

    • Cookies will expire within [30-90 days] unless otherwise specified. Persistent cookies are used only where necessary.

  3. Additional Clauses:

    1. Third-Party Cookies:
      Some cookies may be operated by third parties (e.g., social media platforms, analytics providers). Users are advised to review the privacy policies of such entities.

    2. Data Security:
      Data collected through cookies is encrypted and anonymized where possible.

The Legacy of Pharma pre-connect

Pharma pre-connect Confex is the largest and the most influential pharma packaging show in India. InnoPack is a global brand of CPhI Worldwide and it takes place annually across six regions worldwide.

Launched in 2012, The Pharma pre-connect Confex has served as a platform of advanced knowledge and industry best practices, facilitated partnerships & alliances and serve as a catalyst in shaping the future of the industry.

The year 2020 Pharma pre-connect had embraced the new Era of Packaging as centre of driving business through disruptive innovations as a Virtual Confex.

The 2-day Confex witnessed

 

  • 1000+ Attendees
  • Delegation from 15+ Countries
  • 40+ Pharma Packaging Stalwarts as Speakers
  • 5+ Technical Workshops
  • Packaging Leaders Round Table
  • 5th Annual Pharma Packaging Awards

The Triangle of Justice, Love & Peace!

Powerful words like Global Justice, Love & Peace are the climax of today's Summit;
Involve one's whole soul & together to these words commit.

Spreading these words through this world's largest private event on Global Justice, Love & Peace is our mission;
This is our conveyor Dr Huzaifa Khorakiwalla's foresighted vision.

The language of the world is full of dark harsh actions;
Across our manmade globe sections.

We are living in a forest of missiles, tañks & rockets;
While some of the nations just filling up their pockets.

Come let us join hands to change this blood stained earth;
Let Justice, Love & Peace flow & show its worth.

Inner peace much needed has 7 quotes such as Gratitude, Forgiveness, Loving, Humility, Giving, Patience & Truth;
Let's sow these 7 seeds and relish their fruit,

We assemble here today to bring together voices from around the world in the spirit of understanding & unity;
Come people do bring in a change or for mankind it is truly a pity.

Why then in the struggle of life so many fights for different types of power?
And lo! when he dies, he just exits with a white cover.

A reminder to stay calm and grounded;
Regardless the chaos so we think of the pigeon bird when hounded.

Our philosophy is where every smile counts;
Let's walk together miles ahead and indeed Justice, Love & Peace will mount!

By : Tasneem Nasrulla, India

My eyes are sore seeing the blood stains day and night,

My ears echoes of the loud noises of rockets and missiles,

My nose can only smell gunpowder ,

My mouth is dry by constantly calling for help,

My feet are parched running helter skelter,

My fingers carry pain for they are always wiping tears ,

My whole soul together is in great trauma,

But my heart is the only organ which has some hope,

Hope that one day there will be Justice Peace and Love!

Thus this Global Summit of Justice, Love and Peace is to bring the needed change. So that these voices can have sweet memories of what they are doing, and their heart's desire comes true¡

3
The Silent One

They walk amongst us, but in silent ways,
spreading peace and love without any praise.
When thunder roars and lightning strikes in rain
they watch over us, healing bleeding pain.

Yet we do not see their celestial light,
nor do they shed feathers within our sight.
Happy to hide behind unknown faces,
empathy guides them to deprived places.

Heal and soothe, they prevent tears from flowing,
touch our hearts to leave our spirits glowing.
From Sydney to London, Rome to Bombay,
provide moments that take our breath away

Their acts of grace form a ripple effect,
kind gestures that help people to connect.

By : Ivor Gurney (The Silent One), United Kingdom

A Japanese Poem on Forgiveness:

Beneath autumn skies,

Leaves fall, their whispers softened, Echoes of the past.

A stream forgets stones,

Flowing freely to the sea, So must the heart heal.

In the quiet dawn,

Wounds fade like fleeting shadows, Light embraces all.

By: Alceu Da Silva Moreira, Angola / Canada

5
The Love Bug in the Greek World

Love is believed to be from the creator a precious gift;
It is a blessing to us for our happiness to lift.

Love is not just a simple four letter word to treasure;
But it is hidden with many complexities with which one must measure.

In Greek,love is defined in four different forms;
So I paint it in my poem as a standard norm.

Agape is noted to be love in a spiritual sense,the unconditional love of God for his creation dear;
It can be compared to the illusion from a transparent glass as all very clear.

Euros denotes the passionate,the physical longing & desire in a romantic mood;
It's for the hungry souls the ultimate food.

Philia is a type of general feeling of love between families & friends;
It has lot to do with giving & taking which is the usual trend.

Storge seems to be the natural affection like the love felt by parents for their off springs;
Which together tender caring brings.

Love is like the serene crystal lake;
But once you throw pebbles, ripples it will make.

Love is like a sparkling fire which gives warmth to the cold;
But if played with it, burns will be shown bold.

Love comes to each one of us in different coloured shades;
And as days go by, the colour away to another shade wades.

Love is for each one a beautiful dream;
Let each one find his own beam!

By : Tasneem Nasrulla, India

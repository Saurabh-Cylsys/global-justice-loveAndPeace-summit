Mr. Nadir  B. Godrej

Chairman & Managing Director of  Godrej Industries Ltd.

Mr. Nadir B. Godrej is the Chairman and Managing Director of Godrej Industries Ltd. and Chairman of Godrej Agrovet Ltd. He is also the Co-Chair of Godrej Good & Green Governing Council. He holds a Master of Science degree in chemical engineering from Stanford University and a MBA from Harvard Business School. He has been a Director of several Godrej companies since 1977 and has developed the animal feed, agricultural inputs and chemicals businesses of Godrej Industries and other associate companies, and has been very active in research.

Mr. Godrej is deeply committed to the GOOD and GREEN strategies and achievement of set targets for the Godrej Group.  He encourages and supports a shared vision value for all programs of Good and Green.  

Mr. Godrej is a recipient of many prestigious awards in the country and overseas  including the Hurun award for most respected Indian Industrialist of the Year. He also received a Doctor of Philosophy in Business Management from XIM University, Bhubaneswar in recognition of his contribution to Industry and Society.

He was recently felicitated with  ‘Green Business Titan’ Award for his commitment towards driving sustainability at the Godrej Group, by the JSW The Times of India Earth Care Awards.

Highness Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Ramona Murad

Visionary Leader | Global Ambassador | Advocate
for Human Rights & Economic Development

Her Highness Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Romona Murad (SSAS, DSMP, DMP, SMP, MIPR) of Malaysia embodies multifaceted leadership and is the visionary behind Romona Consultancy and Management, headquartered in the vibrant city of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

In her esteemed capacity, Princess Romona serves as the Ambassador-at-Large for Malaysia under the auspices of the International Human Rights Organization (IHRO), while simultaneously presiding as the Country President for Malaysia within the Economic Council of India. Princess Romona also holds position as Regional Director for the Global Hope Foundation based in Michigan, USA.

Her chairmanship extends to the Malaysian branch of the esteemed Royal Society Group, affiliated with the global network based in Al-Jubail, Saudi Arabia. Her dedication also sees her as a Patron Member of the Euro Asia Financial Policy Forum (EAFPF), anchored in Italy. Princess Romona also performs duties as the Honorary Chairman and Vice-Sovereign of the Sovereign Council of YUDA (Youth United for Development Association) based in Cameroon, Central Africa.

Marking her influence in the international dialogue, Princess Romona was designated as Chairperson for the International Commission for Malaysia by the International Organization for Peace and International Security to Activate International Humanitarian Law for Peace (IOPSH) as of March 2020, further solidifying her role as a pivotal Member of the International Forum in support of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals from April 2020. Princess Romona was also appointed to the position as Honorary Vice-President of the EUMMAS (European Marketing and Management Association) Women’s Forum which is based in BosniaHerzegovina in 2021 based on her involvement in women’s activities and leadership worldwide.

Her Highness's commitment to national representation was fortified by her appointment as RSG Commissioner for Heraldry and Sovereignty to Malaysia and as an Official Delegate by the Royal Society Group World, reflecting the depth of her international relations established since January 2014.

A trailblazer for women, Princess Romona holds the prestigious title of Asia President for the Chamber of Inter Commerce under the International Royal Academy of United Nations (IRAOUN), based in New York. As an Honorary Doctorate Member and the inaugural woman from the Far East in a senior role within the IRAOUN, she weaves a global platform of networks and partnerships.

Her humanitarian spirit shines as she engages with altruistic organizations, including her roles with Fight Cancer Global and Women Who Do Wonders International, and as an Ambassador for the Global Tolerance Faces, Dubai.

Recognition of her tireless humanitarian efforts was heralded by the Global Academy for Human Excellence (GAHE) on International Women's Day 2020 in Manila, bestowing upon her the accolade of Top Women Icon Global 2020. Her advocacy and unwavering dedication to peace, love, and human rights have earned her the affectionate title "Princess of Hearts." Due to her resolute commitment to peace and humanity, Princess Romona also being nominated for "The Medal of Excellence for the Most Influential Figures in the World 2014" by ICHAPS (International Council of Human Rights, Arbitration and Political Studies).

Furthering her commitment to global peace and development, Princess Romona was honoured as a recipient of the "Grand Order of the Noble Hand of Honor" by PALEDEC Africa in recognition of her outstanding virtues and selfless services in protecting the twelve rights of a child as contained in the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child, in Accra, Ghana on 7th Sept 2019. Her Highness also a Board of Advisor for Malaysia for Rockfield College of Sciences and Technology and a Non-Executive Board Member of the Organization for the Economic Reform of Lebanon (based in London, UK).

In her latest groundbreaking role, in 2024, she has been elected by the 360 Leadership Committee of the Marine Foundation to the Prominent First Ladies Club of the Royal Department, embracing a lifelong mandate to amplify the voices of women globally and advocate for the development of institutions and schools dedicated to the wellbeing of children worldwide. Princess Romona also holds position as the President for the Marine Foundation, Malaysia since 2024. Not to mention, Princess Romona has been appointed on 30th October 2024 as an Honorary Country Chairperson for the UNAccc Malaysia. Unity of Nations Action for Climate Change Councils is based in Gujerat, India and it is committed to protect Mother Earth from global warming, and other adverse impacts of climate change.

With her royal hand extended in service, Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Romona Murad continues to be a beacon of hope and an architect of change for communities across the globe.

Rina Telesphore, Dr, His Royal Highness, The Prince

Head of the Royal House of Andriakazomanga-Zafimbolamena MAZAVA

Official Biography of HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore

His Royal Highness Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore exemplifies visionary leadership rooted in heritage and guided by a commitment to progress. As a descendant of King Radama I of Madagascar and King-Priest Andriakazomangabemanefoefo, he blends spiritual and temporal leadership to promote justice, peace, and sustainable development.

Advancing Economic Growth and Sustainability

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore is a prominent advocate for economic progress and international collaboration. As a Board Advisor to the Asian-African Chamber of Commerce and Industry (AACCI), he fosters partnerships between Asia and Africa, encouraging equitable trade and sustainable development. As President-Patron of Trade and Investment for the Commonwealth Entrepreneurs Club, he works to attract responsible investments, strengthen local enterprises, and ensure ethical practices. His contributions to the mining sector, as President of the Association of Jewelers, Lapidaries, and Mining Operators of Madagascar, underscore his dedication to sustainable resource management and community empowerment.

Promoting Diplomacy and Peacebuilding

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore is a strong proponent of global diplomacy and international cooperation. Serving as an Honorary Board Advisor for the World Peace and Diplomacy Organization (WPDO) and a World Peace Ambassador, he actively supports initiatives that resolve conflicts, enhance intercultural understanding, and build bridges between nations. His work reflects a steadfast commitment to justice, human dignity, and harmonious global relations.

Educational and Spiritual Leadership

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore’s exceptional academic achievements include:

  • Professional Doctorate in Global Leadership and Management (European International University, Paris)
  • Doctorate in Business Administration in Banking (Harvest Christian University, USA)
  • Leadership Development Diploma (World Faith Bible Institute)
  • Master Jeweler Certification (HBJO-Paris)

Inheriting the spiritual legacy of King-Priest Andriakazomangabemanefoefo, he was anointed as Senior Pastor in 2012 within the Sanctuaire Chrétien International. This role enables him to integrate values of love, unity, and justice into his leadership, inspiring communities to work together for shared goals.

A Visionary Leader for the Future

As Head of the Royal House of Andriakazomanga-Zafimbolamena MAZAVA, HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore embodies the balance of tradition and modernity. His leadership continues to inspire global efforts for justice, cultural preservation, and shared prosperity, shaping a future founded on unity and enduring harmony.

Awards Research Committee

All 28 Awards are conferred through a non-biased & meritorious process by a 28-membered Awards Research Committee.
These Researchers are across all continents and located in 23 countries and are ably qualified to conduct this process

All nominees identify had made a ‘significant’ impact – but could be in any profession –
Corporate employee, Entrepreneur, Entertainer, Sportsperson, Lawyer, Investor, Wealth Manager, Community Worker, Activist,
Politician, Bureaucrat, Educator, Journalist, Publisher, Health-care Professional, Technocrat, anybody!

Each Awardee has finally been selected based on the following criteria:

  • 1Scale
  • 2Impact
  • 3Longevity
  • 4Innovation
  • 5Sustainability
  • 6Integrity
  • 7Courage

The Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit is committed to the highest level of integrity in the conduct of its Awards Programmes.
No financial consideration has been attached to any Award.

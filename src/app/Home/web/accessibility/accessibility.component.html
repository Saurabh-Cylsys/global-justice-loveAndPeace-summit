ACCESSIBILITY POLICY






1 Accessibility Policy

  • Purpose:

    To ensure our website, events, and services are accessible to all individuals, regardless of ability or circumstance, in compliance with UAE Federal Law No. 29 of 2006, UAE Federal Law No. 45 of 2021 (PDPL), and international standards like WCAG 2.1.

  • Policy:

  1. Digital Accessibility:

    • Website features include compatibility with screen readers, captioning for multimedia content, and adjustable text sizes.

    • Our mobile applications and third-party platforms used for event registration comply with accessibility guidelines.

  2. Physical Accessibility for Events:

    • Venues are chosen with accessible parking, entrances, and seating areas.

    • Sign language interpretation, Braille materials, or live captioning will be provided upon request.

  3. Inclusivity in Content:

    • Event presentations and materials are designed to be inclusive, with alternative formats available on request (e.g., large print, simplified text).

  4. Emergency Preparedness:

    • Safety procedures include considerations for attendees with mobility challenges or sensory impairments

  5. Advanced Notice for Requests:

    • Accessibility requests must be made at least [10] business days before the event to ensure appropriate arrangements.

  6. Review and Accountability:

    • Accessibility policies are reviewed annually. A dedicated accessibility officer ensures compliance and addresses grievances.
